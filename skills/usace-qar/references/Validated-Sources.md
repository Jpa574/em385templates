# Validated Sources for QAR Knowledge

## Primary USACE References

### 1. EM 385-1-1 (Safety Manual)
- **Full Title:** Safety and Occupational Health Requirements Manual
- **Latest:** March 2024 revision
- **Source:** https://www.publications.usace.army.mil/portals/76/publications/engineermanuals/em_385-1-1.pdf
- **Use:** Safety requirements, AHA format, RAC levels

### 2. EP 415-1-261 (QAR Guide Series)
- **Full Title:** Quality Assurance Representative's Guide
- **Volumes:**
  - Vol 01: General Construction
  - Vol 02: Conventional and Precast Concrete Construction
  - Vol 03: Steel and Metal Construction, Welding
  - Vol 04: Special Construction, Conveying Systems, Mechanical, Electrical
  - Vol 05: Retrocommissioning
- **Source:** https://www.publications.usace.army.mil/Portals/76/Publications/EngineerPamphlets/
- **Use:** QAR procedures, inspection requirements, documentation

### 3. UFGS (Specifications)
- **Source:** WBDG - Whole Building Design Guide
- **URL:** https://www.wbdg.org/ffc/dod/unified-facilities-guide-specifications-ufgs
- **Use:** Material specs, installation requirements, testing

### 4. OSHA 1926 (Construction Safety)
- **Source:** Code of Federal Regulations 29 CFR 1926
- **URL:** https://www.osha.gov/laws-regs/regulations/standardnumber/1926
- **Use:** Safety regulations, fall protection, scaffolding, excavations

## AHA Validation Sources (Specific)

### JSABuilder.com
- **URL:** https://jsabuilder.com/resources/activity-hazard-analysis.php
- **Content:** USACE-compatible AHA format, step-by-step procedures, RAC matrix
- **Validated:** AHA definition, format requirements, RAC levels

### Gadzoom.net
- **URL:** https://blog.gadzoom.net/2024/03/13/the-new-em385-is-coming/
- **Content:** 2024 EM 385-1-1 updates, new AHA formatting requirements
- **Validated:** New reference number requirements for steps, hazards, controls

### OSHA Academy
- **URL:** https://www.oshacademy.com/courses/training/510-em385-1-1-safety-health/510-2-5.php
- **Content:** EM 385-1-1 training, control hierarchy
- **Validated:** Control hierarchy (elimination to PPE)

## Note on EP 415-1-261

EP 415-1-261 is the authoritative QAR guide but the PDF is encrypted/secured. We know it exists and contains:
- QAR procedures and responsibilities
- Inspection documentation requirements
- Quality assurance verification processes

For full content, access through USACE publications portal or request from Contracting Officer's Representative.
