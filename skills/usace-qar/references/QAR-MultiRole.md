# QAR Multi-Role Perspective

A QAR must think like multiple roles to effectively manage a project:

---

## 1. Project Engineer (PE) Perspective

**What PE owns:**
- Schedule management
- Submittal tracking and log
- RFI log and responses
- Cost control / pay requests
- Drawing distribution
- Coordination with designer

**What QAR learns from PE:**
- What submittals are critical path
- Schedule impacts of delays
- RFI turnaround expectations
- Drawing revision tracking

**QAR Documentation for PE:**
- Submittal review comments
- RFI impacts noted
- Schedule concerns

---

## 2. Superintendent Perspective

**What Super owns:**
- Day-to-day field operations
- Crew assignment and direction
- Material staging and logistics
- Trade coordination on site
- Sequence of work
- Progress tracking

**What QAR learns from Super:**
- How trades sequence (MEP coordination)
- Where bottlenecks occur
- What areas are active
- Material delivery timing

**QAR Documentation for Super:**
- Work observations by area
- Access needs
- Coordination requests

---

## 3. Quality Control (QC) Manager Perspective

**What QC owns:**
- Inspection implementation
- Testing coordination
- Documentation management
- Pre-cover inspections
- Deficiency tracking
- Commissioning witness

**What QAR shares with QC:**
- All inspection findings
- Test results
- Deficiency status
- Pre-cover signoffs

**QAR Documentation for QC:**
- Daily QC reports
- Test witness documentation
- Pre-cover checklists
- Punch list tracking

---

## 4. Site Safety & Health Officer (SSHO) Perspective

**What SSHO owns:**
- Safety planning (PSCP)
- Hazard analysis (JHA, JSA)
- Training documentation
- Incident reporting
- Daily safety inspections
- PPE compliance

**What QAR shares with SSHO:**
- Safety observations
- Near-miss incidents
- Stop work authority coordination
- Hazard documentation

**QAR Documentation for SSHO:**
- Safety observations in daily report
- Contractor notifications
- Stop work documentation

---

## Daily QAR Thinking

When on site, QAR should ask:

**PE:** "What's submitted? What's coming? Any RFIs out?"
**Super:** "Who's working where? What's the sequence? Any conflicts?"
**QC:** "What's ready to inspect? What test is next? Any open deficiencies?"
**SSHO:** "What's the hazard of the day? PPE adequate? Fire watch needed?"

---

## Integration with Project Phases

### Pre-Con
- PE: Review submittal schedule
- Super: Review sequence plan
- QC: Review inspection / test plan
- SSHO: Review safety plan

### Construction
- PE: Track submittals, RFIs, schedule
- Super: Coordinate daily work
- QC: Daily inspections, testing
- SSHO: Safety observations

### Closeout
- PE: Ensure documentation complete
- Super: Punch list management
- QC: Final inspections, commissioning
- SSHO: Safety closeout
