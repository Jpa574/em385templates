# UFGS 32 00 00 - Paving and Surfacing

## Key Spec Highlights for QAR

### Materials
- **Asphalt:** Performance grade (PG)
- **Concrete:** Air content, strength per mix design
- **Base:** Aggregate base, crush stone

### Critical Inspection Points

1. **Subgrade/Base
   - Compaction verified
   - Proper grade and drainage
   - No soft spots

2. **Asphalt Paving
   - Temperature at placement
   - Compaction (density achieved)
   - Joints (tack coat, proper overlap)

3. **Concrete Paving
   - Concrete temperature
   - Joint spacing
   - Curing method
   - Surface finish

### Documentation Required
- Mix designs
- Temperature logs
- Density test reports
- Jointing plan

### Common Deficiencies
- Segregation
- Poor compaction
- Wrong PG grade
- Cold joints
- Improper curing
- Surface too rough
