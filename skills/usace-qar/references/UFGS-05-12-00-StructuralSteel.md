# UFGS 05 12 00 - Structural Steel

## Key Spec Highlights for QAR

### Materials
- **Wide Flange/WT:** ASTM A992
- **Angles, Channels, Plates:** ASTM A36
- **Tubing:** ASTM A500 Grade C or A1085
- **Pipe:** ASTM A53 Grade B
- **Anchor Rods:** ASTM F1554 Gr 36/55/105

### Critical Inspection Points

1. **Fabrication**
   - Punch/subpunch and ream or drill perpendicular to surface
   - Compression joints: surface roughness max 13 micrometer
   - Shop primer: SSPC SP 6 (blast clean), 2.0 mil DFT min

2. **Erection**
   - Follow AISC 325/ANSI AISC 303
   - Temporary shoring and bracing per erection drawings
   - Alignment within tolerances

3. **Connections**
   - Bolts: ASTM A307 (common) or A325 (high-strength)
   - Proper installation method (turn-of-nut, DTI, etc.)
   - Bearing type vs slip-critical connections

4. **Welding**
   - AWS D1.1 compliance
   - Welder certification current
   - Visual inspection + NDT per spec

### Documentation Required
- Shop drawings (erection marks, splice locations)
- Mill certifications (steel, bolts)
- Welder certs (current within 6 months)
- NDT reports (UT, MT, PT)
- Paint certifications

### Common Deficiencies
- Wrong bolt type (A307 vs A325)
- Improper bolt tensioning
- Missing bridge rails (top of column)
- Wrong primer thickness
- Welding without proper certs
- Misalignment beyond tolerance
- Missing drainage holes in sealed sections
