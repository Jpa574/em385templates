# OSHA Safety - QAR Responsibilities

## QAR Role in Safety

QAR does NOT enforce safety — that's the Contractor's duty. But QAR must:
- Stop work for imminent danger
- Report unsafe conditions to contractor
- Document safety concerns in daily reports

---

## Common Safety Issues on USACE Projects

### Fall Protection
- Guardrails in place
- Floor openings covered
- Personal fall arrest systems

### Scaffolding
- Proper construction per OSHA
- Guardrails, midrails, toe boards
- Access proper

### Electrical
- GFCI protection
- Temporary wiring proper
- No improvised setups

### Excavations
- Shoring in place
- Access/egress provided
- Soil classified

---

## Documentation

- Note safety concerns in daily report
- Do NOT direct contractor on corrective action
- Report to COR/PM for safety issues

---

*Reference: 29 CFR 1926 (Construction Safety)*
