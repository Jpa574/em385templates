# UFGS 22 00 00 - Plumbing, General Purpose

## Key Spec Highlights for QAR

### Materials
- **Pipe:** Multiple types per spec - cast iron, copper, plastic (PVC/CPVC), steel
- **Fittings:** Match pipe material, correct joining method
- **Valves:** Brass or bronze, rated for service
- **Fixtures:** Water closets, lavatories, urinals, sinks per spec

### Critical Inspection Points

1. **Underground**
   - Pipe material and bedding
   - Joints proper (bell-and-spigot, no-hub, weld)
   - Slope for drainage
   - Leak testing (pressure test)

2. **Aboveground**
   - Pipe supports per MSS SP-58
   - Insulation (hot water, cold water, drain lines)
   - Flashings at penetrations
   - Fixture mounting and connection

3. **Testing**
   - Drainage: hydrostatic test or smoke test
   - Water supply: pressure test
   - Gas: soap test or pressure test

### Documentation Required
- Shop drawings (piping layout)
- Product data (all fixtures, valves, pipe)
- Test reports (pressure tests)
- Fixture certifications

### Common Deficiencies
- Wrong pipe material for application
- Missing insulation on domestic water
- Improper slope on drainage
- No air gap at fixtures
- Missing cleanouts
- Valve not accessible
- Pipe not properly supported
