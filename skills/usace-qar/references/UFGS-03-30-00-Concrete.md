# UFGS 03 30 00 - Cast-In-Place Concrete

## Key Spec Highlights for QAR

### Materials
- **Cement:** Per spec type (I, II, III, etc.), mill certs required
- **Aggregates:** ASTM C33, gradation per spec
- **Admixtures:** Per manufacturer's specs, compatible with mix
- **Water:** Clean, potable, no impurities
- **Reinforcement:** ASTM A615 or A706, epoxy-coated where required

### Critical Inspection Points

1. **Pre-Concrete Placement**
   - Reinforcement placement, spacing, ties
   - Concrete cover (min per spec, typically 1.5" - 3")
   - Formwork condition and cleanliness
   - Vapor retarder/barrier installed properly
   - Embedded items (sleeves, conduits) in place
   - Waterstops at joints

2. **During Placement**
   - Concrete temperature (cold/hot weather limits)
   - Slump verification
   - Placement method (no free fall >5')
   - Consolidation (vibration)

3. **Testing**
   - Compressive strength: 4"x8" cyl per ASTM C31/C39
   - Slump per ASTM C143
   - Air content (if exposed to freeze-thaw)
   - Temperature

4. **Curing**
   - Method per spec (water, membrane, steam)
   - Duration (typically 7 days min, 14 for structural)
   - Protection from damage

### Documentation Required
- Mix design (60 days before placement)
- Mill test reports (cement, aggregates)
- Shop drawings (reinforcement bending)
- Test reports (compressive strength)
- Curing plan

### Common Deficiencies
- Incorrect cover thickness
- Reinforcement not tied (spacing wrong)
- Cold joint (placement delay)
- Improper curing (cracking)
- Missing vapor barrier
- Formwork not properly braced
- Concrete placement in wrong weather
