# ADA Compliance - QAR Quick Reference

## Americans with Disabilities Act

QAR must verify ADA compliance throughout. Key areas:

### Accessible Routes
- **Walkways:** 48" min width, cross slope ≤2%
- **Ramps:** Max slope 1:12, handrails both sides
- **Doors:** 32" clear opening, hardware accessible

### Bathrooms
- **Toilets:** 17-19" height, grab bars per spec
- **Lavatories:** 34" max height, clear knee space
- **Accessories:** Paper towel, mirror heights

### Signage
- **Braille:** Per ADA requirements
- **Mounting height:** 48" to baseline
- **Character size:** Per distance

### Parking
- **Spaces:** Van accessible (8' wide + 8' access aisle)
- **Signage:** Proper identification
- **Accessible route to building

---

## QAR Inspection Notes

- Verify during framing, before walls close
- Document any non-conformance
- Include in punch list if not corrected

---

*Reference: ICC A117.1, ADA Standards for Accessible Design*
